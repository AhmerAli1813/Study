1. In the following verse, the word “بُهْتَانًا” means:
Correct Answer: False Accusation

2. What was the relation of Hazrat Abdullah bin Umar with Umm ul Momineen Hazrat Hafsa R.A?
Correct Answer: Brother

3. What is the real name of Imam Tirmidhi?
Correct Answer: Muhammad Bin Esa

4. What is the book of hadith compiled from jurisprudential order called?
Correct Answer: Sunan

5. According to the following verse, in what manners do the believers walk on earth?
وَعِبَادُ الرَّحْمَنِ الَّذِينَ يَمْشُونَ عَلَى الْأَرْضِ هَوْنًا
Correct Answer: Humbly

1. Who purchased Hazrat Zaid bin Haritha for Hazrat Khadija (RA)?
   - Correct Answer: Hakim bin Hizam (the nephew of Hazrat Khadija RA) purchased Hazrat Zaid bin Haritha in a market and later gifted him to his aunt Hazrat Khadija (RA). She then gifted Zaid to Prophet Muhammad (PBUH).

2. The verse shown in the image is from Surah Al-Furqan (25:74), which means:
   "And those who say, 'Our Lord, grant us from among our wives and offspring comfort to our eyes and make us a leader for the righteous (the pious)."
   - Correct Answer: ✅ The Pious.

3. The correct answer to the question about the compilation of the hadith collection is:
   - Correct Answer: ✅ Sahifa al Sadiqa (صحيفه الصادقه).
   Hamaam bin Munabbih compiled the hadith collection known as Sahifa al Sadiqa, one of the earliest known collections of hadith.

   "And those who harm believing men and believing women for something they did not do, they bear (the burden of) a false accusation and manifest sin."
   - Correct Answer: ✅ Bears the burden of a false allegation.
